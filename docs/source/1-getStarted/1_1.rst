Platform Structure 
==================

The platform comprises multiple pages allowing the users to publish and manage the data. Here, particular attention is devoted to the data uploading and editing of the layers and creating users and groups. For this reason, the documentation will focus on the `Layers`, `Users` and `Groups` page.

.. note::

    If the users want to become familiar with the different pages of the site, they are more than welcome to explore the platform.
    
    For additional documentation of the different pages the users are ecourage to visit `GeoNode Users Guide <https://docs.geonode.org/en/master/usage/index.html>`_.
